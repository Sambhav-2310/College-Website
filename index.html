Christ University is a deemed private university located in Bangalore, India. Established in 1969, the university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines. With state-of-the-art facilities, a diverse student community, and a commitment to academic excellence, Christ University has emerged as one of the top educational institutions in India.
